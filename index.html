CONTROLS

  • [esc]: show / hide help
  • [space]: pause / play
  • [enter]: next step
  • [<--]: reset to random
  • [del]: reset to empty
  • [LMB]: draw alive cell
  • [shift+LMB]: erase cell
  • [Mouse Wheel]: zoom
  • [RMB]: pan around
  • [h]: toggle history shader
  • [1]...[9]: change brush size
  • [F11]: fullscreen
  • [c][s][r]: change brush shape
